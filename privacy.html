Privacy Policy

Effective: 19 July 2026

Short version: Beacon Bay does not require an account, contains no adverts or cross-app tracking, and keeps game progress on your device.

Information we collect

Beacon Bay does not send personal information, gameplay analytics, advertising identifiers, contacts, photos, location or device activity to the developer. The game does not require registration or a player account.

Game progress stored on your device

Level progress, settings, coins, boosters and purchase-delivery records are stored locally on your device. This information is used only to run the game, restore your current board and prevent the same StoreKit transaction being delivered twice.

Deleting Beacon Bay may permanently remove locally stored progress and virtual items. Version 1.0 does not provide cloud save or cross-device progress transfer.

In-app purchases

Optional purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. Apple may collect and process purchase, account and payment information under Apple’s Privacy Policy. Beacon Bay receives only the StoreKit transaction information necessary to deliver the purchased virtual item and prevent duplicate delivery. The developer does not operate a purchase-history server.

Advertising, analytics and tracking

Beacon Bay contains no advertising SDK, third-party analytics SDK or technology used to track you across apps or websites.

Children’s privacy

Beacon Bay does not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults. If the game’s data practices change, this policy and the App Store privacy information will be updated before the change is released.

Your choices and deletion

You can erase game progress from the level map or remove all locally stored game information by deleting the app. Because there is no Beacon Bay account or developer-operated player database, there is no server-side profile to access or delete.

Changes to this policy

The effective date above will change whenever this policy is materially updated. The current policy will remain available at this address.

Contact

Questions about privacy or the game can be emailed to satisfyinglycute@gmail.com or sent through the Beacon Bay support page.
