Player Support

Keeper Finn is here to help with gameplay, purchases and saved progress.

Before contacting support
  • Close and reopen Beacon Bay.
  • Confirm your device is online if a purchase is pending.
  • Do not delete the app while troubleshooting; version 1.0 stores progress locally.

Report a problem

Email satisfyinglycute@gmail.com and describe what happened, your iPhone model, iOS version and the Beacon Bay level. Never include payment details, passwords or Apple Account credentials.

Email Beacon Bay support

You can alternatively open a public GitHub support request. Do not include private information in a public issue.

Purchase problems

If Apple completed a purchase but the item has not appeared, reopen Beacon Bay while online so StoreKit can retry delivery. For refunds or Apple billing questions, use Apple’s Report a Problem service.

Saving and restoring progress

Beacon Bay version 1.0 saves progress on the current device. Deleting the app can remove progress, coins and boosters. Cloud save and cross-device transfer are not currently available.
